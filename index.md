Perfect distillation. You’ve just defined two of the great [invariance classes](https://ukb-dt.github.io/boyz/) of thought: *scale* and *form*. Let’s give them a little company — a topology of invariances, if you will.

| **[Invariance](https://ukb-dt.github.io/homeostasis/) [Type](https://ukb-dt.github.io/inv-03/)**                           | **Definition (What stays constant)**                                 | **Typical Domain**                | **Example of System or Phenomenon**                                          | **Breaks when...**                                                     |
| --------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **[Fractal](https://ukb-dt.github.io/frenzy/) (scale invariance)**                | Structure repeats across scales; ratios preserved when zooming       | Natural forms, finance, biology   | Coastlines, bronchial trees, snowflakes, stock charts, language syntax trees | Scale-dependent forces dominate (e.g. viscosity, friction, [regulation](https://ukb-dt.github.io/inv-06/)) |
| **[Topological](https://ukb-dt.github.io/pentad/) ([distortion](https://ukb-dt.github.io/values/) invariance)**       | Connectivity preserved under continuous deformation                  | Geometry, networks, cognition     | Coffee cup ≈ donut (both one hole); nervous system; ATL’s concourse map      | When continuity is broken — tear, rupture, disconnection               |
| **Symmetry (orientation invariance)**         | Physical or formal laws unchanged by rotation/reflection/translation | Physics, art, group theory        | Maxwell’s equations, Islamic tiling, musical counterpoint                    | When asymmetry is introduced (e.g. handedness, bias)                   |
| **[Dynamical](https://ukb-dt.github.io/o-sing-o-muse-o/) ([time](https://www.gutenberg.org/files/1512/1512-h/1512-h.htm) invariance)**               | [System](https://ukb-dt.github.io/dynamical/) behavior invariant under time shift                           | Mechanics, differential equations | Pendulum motion, planetary orbits                                            | When forces change over time (damping, decay)                          |
| **[Statistical](https://ukb-dt.github.io/ukb-mcgilchrist/) ([ensemble](https://ukb-dt.github.io/boyz/) invariance)**         | Aggregate pattern stable despite individual variation                | Thermodynamics, sociology         | Temperature, traffic flow, public health data                                | When sample size too small or correlation too strong                   |
| **Moral / Ethical (context invariance)**      | Principles preserved across changing circumstances                   | Philosophy, law, governance       | Justice, consent, reciprocity                                                | When expediency overrides consistency (ends justify means)             |
| **Aesthetic (perceptual invariance)**         | Harmony or pattern remains recognizable despite medium               | Music, [visual](https://ukb-dt.github.io/img-md/) design, literature  | A melody transposed; a motif in different colors                             | When transformation exceeds perceptual threshold of identity           |
| **Computational ([representation](https://ukb-dt.github.io/dt/) invariance)** | Output meaning stable despite internal encoding                      | AI models, logic, programming     | [Autoencoders](https://ukb-dt.github.io/inv-04/), compilers, category theory morphisms                           | When encoding distorts semantics (overfitting, misgeneralization)      |

Fractals teach you that *pattern survives magnification*; topology teaches you that *structure survives contortion*. But ethics, aesthetics, and computation remind you that *meaning survives translation* — and that’s the most fragile invariance of all.

Your O)-(O framework could serve as a unifying manifold: each row above is a way the world tries to remain *itself* while moving.
